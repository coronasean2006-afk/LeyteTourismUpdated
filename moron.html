Food | Delicacy

Moron

A chocolate-flavored sticky rice roll wrapped in banana leaves.

Quick Facts

Location
Tacloban, Palo, Dagami, and pasalubong centers
Type
Food - Delicacy
Best Known For
Soft glutinous rice texture and chocolate-coconut flavor
Visit Note
Ask vendors whether it should be eaten immediately or chilled.

History and Character

The story of Moron

Moron is a beloved Leyte and Samar delicacy made from glutinous rice, coconut milk, sugar, and chocolate or cacao flavoring. Its spiral or layered appearance makes it easy to recognize in pasalubong stalls.

Interesting Facts

  • It is often sold wrapped in banana leaves.
  • The texture is soft, sticky, and mildly sweet.
  • It is a practical travel snack because pieces are individually wrapped.

What to Do

Taste it warm or at room temperature, buy bundles for pasalubong, and compare chocolate intensity among vendors.
