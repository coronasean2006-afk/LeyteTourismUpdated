Destination | Mountain

Alto Peak

A serious highland hike for prepared travelers who want Leyte's mountain side.

Quick Facts

Location
Ormoc and Jaro trail areas, Leyte
Type
Destination - Mountain
Best Known For
Highland trekking and wide mountain views
Visit Note
Hire a guide and avoid hiking in severe weather.

History and Character

The story of Alto Peak

Alto Peak is part of the mountainous backbone that gives Leyte its rugged interior. While Leyte is often marketed through islands and memorials, hikes like Alto Peak reveal the province's forested and elevated terrain.

Interesting Facts

  • The trail is better suited to prepared hikers than casual walkers.
  • Mountain weather can change quickly even when the coast is clear.
  • Views depend on cloud cover, so early starts help.

What to Do

Hike, walk ridges, photograph highland views, and experience Leyte's cooler interior.
