Roscas

Visit Note

History and Character

The story of Roscas

Roscas are ring-shaped cookies found in Leyte pasalubong shops. Their shape, crunch, and shelf life make them easy to carry home, and they sit comfortably beside sweeter sticky delicacies like moron and binagol.

Interesting Facts

  • Roscas are usually dry, crisp, and lightly sweet.
  • Their ring shape makes them visually distinct from other native snacks.
  • They are convenient for travelers because they keep better than many soft delicacies.

What to Do

Buy them for snacks, pair them with coffee, and include them in a mixed Leyte pasalubong box.
