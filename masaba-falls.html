Masaba Falls

A quieter waterfall stop for travelers looking for cool water and rural inland scenery.

Quick Facts

Location
Matag-ob, Leyte
Type
Destination - Waterfall
Best Known For
Refreshing waterfall scenery in western Leyte
Visit Note
Avoid waterfall swimming after heavy rain.

History and Character

The story of Masaba Falls

Masaba Falls is part of Matag-ob's inland nature appeal. Like many local waterfalls in Leyte, it is shaped by community access, seasonal water flow, and travelers who prefer less crowded natural stops.

Interesting Facts

  • Waterfall flow can change depending on recent rain.
  • Local advice is important because trails and swimming conditions can be seasonal.
  • The stop adds inland variety to western Leyte island itineraries.

What to Do

Take a short hike, view the falls, swim only when safe, and enjoy the shaded setting.
